\# Functional Requirements for SRH Chatbot Mobile App (KASA)

\## Ranked from Highest to Lowest Priority



:



\*\*\*



\## \*\*CRITICAL PRIORITY\*\* (Must Have - Top 10)



These are non-negotiable features that define the core value proposition and address youth safety, privacy, and basic functionality:



1\. \*\*AI-powered conversational chatbot with NLP\*\* - Core chatbot intelligence for natural conversations

2\. \*\*End-to-end encryption\*\* - Protects all user data and conversations

3\. \*\*Anonymous chat (no registration required)\*\* - Removes barriers to access

4\. \*\*Crisis detection and emergency referral\*\* - Identifies distress signals and provides immediate help

5\. \*\*Medically-validated SRH content database\*\* - Ensures accuracy and trustworthiness

6\. \*\*Multi-platform support\*\* (Android, iOS, web) - Maximizes reach

7\. \*\*Offline mode with basic content\*\* - Works without internet connectivity

8\. \*\*Quick exit/panic button\*\* - Allows users to hide app instantly for safety

9\. \*\*Age-appropriate content filtering\*\* - Delivers suitable information based on user age

10\. \*\*Local language support\*\* - English, Twi, Ewe, Hausa for cultural relevance



\*\*\*



\## \*\*HIGH PRIORITY\*\* (Should Have - 11-20)



Features that significantly enhance user experience, safety, and mental health integration:



11\. \*\*Mental health self-assessment tools\*\* - Screens for depression, anxiety, stress

12\. \*\*Geolocation-based health facility finder\*\* - Connects users to nearby clinics

13\. \*\*Push notifications for reminders\*\* - Keeps users engaged with appointments

14\. \*\*In-app emergency hotline directory\*\* - Quick access to crisis lines

15\. \*\*User consent and privacy management\*\* - Transparent data handling

16\. \*\*Conversation history with auto-delete\*\* - User control over data retention

17\. \*\*Voice-to-text input\*\* - Accessibility for users with typing difficulties

18\. \*\*Text-to-speech output\*\* - Supports visually impaired users

19\. \*\*Quick reply buttons\*\* - Speeds up common queries

20\. \*\*Content search and filtering\*\* - Easy navigation through information



\*\*\*



\## \*\*MEDIUM PRIORITY\*\* (Could Have - 21-28)



Enhanced features that improve engagement and personalization:



21\. \*\*Personalized health recommendations\*\* - AI-driven custom advice

22\. \*\*Anonymous peer-to-peer Q\&A forum\*\* - Community support

23\. \*\*Educational multimedia library\*\* - Videos, infographics, podcasts

24\. \*\*Interactive quizzes and gamification\*\* - Makes learning fun

25\. \*\*Mood tracking and journaling\*\* - Mental health monitoring

26\. \*\*Appointment booking integration\*\* - Direct clinic scheduling

27\. \*\*Medication and health reminders\*\* - Treatment adherence support

28\. \*\*User feedback and rating system\*\* - Continuous improvement mechanism



\*\*\*



\## \*\*LOW PRIORITY\*\* (Won't Have in V1 - 29-35)



Nice-to-have features for future versions:



29\. \*\*Social media sharing (anonymous stories)\*\* - Community storytelling

30\. \*\*In-app rewards and achievement badges\*\* - Gamification enhancements

31\. \*\*Multi-device synchronization\*\* - Cross-device experience

32\. \*\*Chatbot personality customization\*\* - Personalized bot character

33\. \*\*Community challenges and campaigns\*\* - Group engagement activities

34\. \*\*Integration with wearable devices\*\* - Health data from smartwatches

35\. \*\*Virtual support group scheduling\*\* - Organized peer support sessions



\*\*\*



\## Key Insights from Prioritization



\*\*MVP Focus (Version 1):\*\*

\- \*\*12 Must Have\*\* requirements form your Minimum Viable Product

\- \*\*8 Should Have\*\* features enhance competitive advantage

\- Focus on \*\*Critical + High Priority\*\* (20 features) for the competition prototype



\*\*Judging Criteria Alignment:\*\*

\- \*\*Technical Feasibility\*\*: Features 1, 2, 6, 15 demonstrate solid architecture

\- \*\*Innovation\*\*: Features 4, 11, 21, 22 showcase creative solutions

\- \*\*Accuracy \& Youth Friendliness\*\*: Features 5, 8, 9, 10 address core needs

\- \*\*UX \& Accessibility\*\*: Features 3, 17, 18, 19 ensure inclusivity

\- \*\*Sustainability\*\*: Features 7, 12, 14, 26 enable long-term viability
